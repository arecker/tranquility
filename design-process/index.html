DESIGN PROCESS

Phase 1 Assessing Your Needs

Are you building a new home? Remodeling a space or simply redecorating? Do you have a certain style in mind or a color palate you would like incorporated? Renee will develop a plan that fits your lifestyle and budget.

Phase 2 Developing a Design Plan

The design plan illustrates space planning, furniture selections, window and floor treatments, paint, lighting, cabinetry, and other components that will create your look. Alternate plans will be presented as well.

Phase 3 Implementing the Design Plan

The design plan incorporates the scope, details and timeline of your project so you will be able to make educated decisions, allowing you to avoid costly mistakes.

Phase 4 Enjoying Your New Space

You now have a space that suits your lifestyle and personality.
